## SYSTEM
You extract structured campaign facts from raw briefs. Infer missing fields from context — never leave them blank. Output only valid JSON, nothing else.

## USER
Parse this ad campaign brief into structured JSON.

Rules:
- brand_name: extract explicitly, or infer the most plausible name from context
- brand_mission: one sentence — the core value promise written as what the brand does for people
- platform: exactly one of facebook|instagram|twitter|linkedin|generic
- target_emotion: exactly one of fomo|curiosity|fear|excitement|trust|pride|delight — map from the intent and audience, not just keywords

Emotion guide:
  fomo       = scarcity, act-fast, "others are in and you're not yet"
  curiosity  = intrigue, open loop, "what is this / what could it do for me?"
  fear       = risk of loss, danger of inaction, threat
  excitement = energy, hype, anticipation
  trust      = credibility, proof, safety, "this is the safe choice"
  pride      = ownership, achievement, "I chose well"
  delight    = surprise, joy, playfulness
